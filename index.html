Chicken Noodle Soup

  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Ingredient

  1. 4 cups of chopped chicken meat
  2. 8 oz egg nooldes
  3. 1 cup of celery
  4. 1/4 cup of carrots
  5. 1/4 cup of onions
  6. 1/4 stick butter
  7. 12 cups of water
  8. 9 cubes of chicken boullion
  9. 1/2 teaspoon dried marjoram
  10. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  11. 1 bay leaf
  12. 1 tablespoon parsely

Directions

  1. In a large stock pot saute celery and onions in butter
  2. Add all ingredients except noodles, simmer for 30 mins
  3. Add noodles and simmer for 10 mins
